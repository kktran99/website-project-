NEPTUNE NITE | 2022 Music Merchandise Tickets FAQ

FAQ

Where is the event located?

The festival will take place at San Jose State University at the Event Center, the address is 1 Washington Sq, San Jose, CA 95192.

How much are the tickets?

Tickets are $80 excluding fees for our one-day-only event and can be purchased under the “Tickets” tab.

Are tickets expected to sell out?

There is always a chance that the event will sell out especially since Neptune Nite is a one-night event, in which case no more tickets will be sold. So, get your tickets now—we want to make sure you don’t miss out!

Where can I park?

Event parking is located in the South Garage (across from the Spartan Recreational Aquatic Center SRAC) and includes pay machines.

What time does the event start and end?

Doors will open at 5 pm, the first artist will take the stage at 6 pm and the last artist will take the stage at 1 am. The event will end approximately at 2 am. For a more detailed setlist please visit the “Music” tab.

When do doors close?

The doors do not have a “close” time, as long as you are holding a ticket that has not yet been scanned that day, you may enter any time during the event. Ins & outs are not allowed.

Is vaccination required to attend?

To protect fellow festival attendees from the risk of COVID-19, vaccination will be required to attend the event or a negative COVID-19 test 48 hours prior. The event will require physical proof and masks are strongly recommended. For vaccination information and centers, please visit your local county health website.

Will there be food at the event?

Since Neptune Nite will be located in the heart of Downtown San Jose and to optimize the limited space available, we will sell basic concessions as other options are available in a close radius.

What is the bag policy?

While we would prefer that fans come bag-free, we realize that is not always possible. Help us to help you enjoy the festival by only bringing the following types and size bags:

Backpacks and bags larger than 6” x 8” x 3” made of clear plastic, clear vinyl, or clear PVC

Small bags, fanny-packs, purses up to 6” X 8” X 3” (do not have to be clear)

Empty dedicated hydration packs with a bladder capacity of under 2.5L/150 in. and no more than two additional pockets. Standard size backpacks with hydration bladders will not be permitted.

What are my ticket options if Neptune Nite must be postponed/rescheduled or canceled?

If Festival is Postponed/Rescheduled: Hold on to your tickets! They will automatically be honored at the rescheduled date. Should you be unable to attend the rescheduled date, you must request a refund within 30 days of the announced new dates. For refund instructions please contact

tickets@neptunenite.com
